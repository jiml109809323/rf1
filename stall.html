Make Your Own Charity Stall

How to Set Up Your Stall

Setting up your charity stall is easy and fun! Start by laying out a table and covering it with a colourful cloth to make it look inviting. Next, place a jar or box where people can safely put their money donations. Then, arrange your food neatly on the table using the recipes below so everything looks tasty and exciting. Most importantly, have fun, smile, and enjoy helping others while raising money for a great cause! 😊
